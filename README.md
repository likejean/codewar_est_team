Common denominators

You will have a list of rationals in the form:
    { {numer_1, denom_1} , ... {numer_n, denom_n} } 
    or
    [ [numer_1, denom_1] , ... [numer_n, denom_n] ] 
    or
    [ (numer_1, denom_1) , ... (numer_n, denom_n) ] 

where all numbers are positive ints. You have to produce a result in the form:
    (N_1, D) ... (N_n, D) 
    or
    [ [N_1, D] ... [N_n, D] ] 
    or
    [ (N_1', D) , ... (N_n, D) ] 
    or
    {{N_1, D} ... {N_n, D}} 
    or
    "(N_1, D) ... (N_n, D)"
